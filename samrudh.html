Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam

Missile Man of India | Former President of India

Biography

Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam was one of India's most respected scientists and the 11th President of India. He played a key role in India's missile development and nuclear weapons program. Because of his contributions, he is popularly known as the "Missile Man of India".

Achievements

  • 11th President of India (2002–2007)
  • Key contributor to India's Pokhran-II nuclear tests
  • Recipient of Bharat Ratna (India’s highest civilian award)
  • Renowned aerospace scientist at ISRO and DRDO

Famous Quote

"Dream, dream, dream. Dreams transform into thoughts and thoughts result in action."
